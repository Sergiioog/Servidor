Frequently Asked Questions

How can we help you?

Car Issues

If you encounter engine issues with your Bentley, we recommend contacting our customer support immediately at +1 (234) 567-89. Our team will guide you through troubleshooting steps or schedule a service appointment to address the problem promptly.

Electronic system issues can be complex, but you can start by checking the vehicle's manual for troubleshooting tips. If the problem persists, reach out to our technical support team at support@bentley.com for personalized assistance.

Suspension issues require professional attention. Contact our service center to schedule an inspection. Visit our website or call +1 (234) 567-89 for assistance.

Website Issues

If you're experiencing login issues on our website, try resetting your password. If the problem persists, contact our website support team at web_support@bentley.com for assistance in resolving the login problem.

If you're unable to access specific features on our website, it could be due to browser compatibility or technical issues. Please ensure your browser is up-to-date. If the problem persists, contact our website support team for further assistance.

To report a bug on our website, please email details to bugs@bentley.com. Include a description of the issue, steps to reproduce it, and any screenshots if possible. Your feedback helps us improve our online experience.
